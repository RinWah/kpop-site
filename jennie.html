background

jennie was born on jan 16, 1996 in seongnam (bundang), sk. she moved to new zealand when she turned 8 and studied at acg parnell and also became fluent in english. around 2010 she auditioned for yg entertainment as a trainee and got accepted and began her training. people describe her as very chic, but still having very child-like characteristics.

trainee era

during jennie's trainee years [6 yrs], she gained visibility through teasers in g-dragon songs and videos. if you don't alrdy know, trainees are evaluated on a monthly basis and are judged by yk producers as they cover various songs with their own choreography, outfits, and sometimes they are even evaluated on their ability to vocally cover the songs as well. most trainees describe it as 'cruel' or cold-hearted places where it's hard to maintain friends and other activities outside of training. it's a very competitive atmosphere where almost anyone is your enemy. trainees frequently have to abandon their homes and live in a dorm within the training grounds. during a youtube series later down the line with blackpink, jennie mentioned that she helped fellow blackpink member rose learn basic dance moves, which helped them grow closer.

blackpink era

on aug 8, 2016, jennie debuted with blackpink as a main rapper and lead vocalist. they released many popular songs tgt as a group, such as 'how you like that', 'kill this love', and 'pink venom'.

solo era

comically alike her song called 'solo', jennie had already established a solo identity apart from blackpink in nov 2018 with her solo single called 'solo'.
