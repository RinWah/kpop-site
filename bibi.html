bibi

i got into bibi after listening to BIBI vengeance. i was obsessed with her vocal tone and her song lyrics were meaningful and sometimes funny since they sounded a lot more romantic or funny then they actually meant the total opposite. her music is such a fun combination of serious songs and playful songs with a diverse range of vocal talent and artistic touch.

trainee era

bibi actually didn't start out like the other k-pop soloists on this list. she began making her own music on SoundCloud and was eventually signed in 2017 under Feel Ghood Music. after running as a participant in a song competition, she gained more traction and popularity with her catchy songs like 'binu'.

solo era

rose had started her solo career with 'gone' and then after the group took a more formal hiatus, released her whole rosie album, which she collaborated with Bruno Mars to release APT. the song went viral and she gained more traction on her other songs and was more recognized for her vocal talent, which is unfortunately more uncommon with the rise of visuals in the k-pop world.
